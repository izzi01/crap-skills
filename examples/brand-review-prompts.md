# Brand Review Example Prompts

## 1. Education landing page sludge
"Review this Vietnamese study-abroad landing page for parents and students. The hero has a purple-blue mesh gradient, glowing CTAs, stock student photography, floating glass cards, and a headline about 'nâng tầm tương lai'. The page talks about dreams and transformation but never explains what happens after contact. Give me a blunt brand review and concrete copy + visual edits."

## 2. AI startup template hero
"Critique this B2B AI operations homepage: dark navy, neon glow, floating dashboard, headline about orchestrating intelligent outcomes, six feature cards, fake trust logos, and a 'Book Demo' CTA. I think it looks like every AI startup site from the last two years. Review it harshly."

## 3. Variant collapse
"I have two homepage variants with different art direction, but the copy for both says personalized journey, expert guidance, clear roadmap, trusted support. Review them like a skeptical brand director and tell me how to differentiate both copy and visuals."

## 4. Strong copy, weak surface
"The homepage copy is actually strong: named audience, clear process, proof, hotline, and teacher bios. But the page is overloaded with glows, giant rounded cards, decorative pills, blur backdrops, and multiple gradients. Review it without flattening the good message layer."

## 5. Strong surface, weak copy
"The visual direction is distinctive—monochrome photography, sharp type, asymmetry, no gradients—but the copy is all 'unlock your potential' and 'personalized support for every step of your journey'. Keep what is visually ownable, but be brutal about the messaging."

## 6. Repo-native test angle
"We have Astro homepage variants in a repo and people keep saying 'this feels too AI-generated'. Turn that into a concrete brand review and then propose repo-native Vitest checks for audience naming, proof density, generic filler, and route collapse."
