# Visual Style Guide Copy Checklist

## Before writing
- Who will use this guide?
- Is this a full guide or a focused section?
- What brand assets or decisions already exist?
- Which rules need examples to avoid drift?

## During writing
- Is the guide scannable?
- Are rules concrete enough to apply?
- Are examples included where misuse is likely?
- Is the prose clean and not marketing-fluffy?
- Does each section help a real team member use the brand system?

## Red flags
- empty brand-story puffery
- too much admiration, not enough guidance
- no do/don't constraints
- no audience or usage context
- rules with no examples and no rationale
