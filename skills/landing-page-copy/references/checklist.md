# Landing Page Copy Checklist

## Before writing
- Who is the page for?
- What problem or decision are they facing?
- What does the offer actually do?
- What proof exists?
- What action should they take next?

## During writing
- Is the audience named clearly?
- Is the offer understandable in the first screen?
- Is the mechanism visible, not just the promise?
- Is proof doing real work?
- Does the CTA match the actual commitment level?

## Red flags
- generic promise language
- feature dump with no reader value
- proofless claims
- fake urgency
- CTA text that says nothing about outcome
