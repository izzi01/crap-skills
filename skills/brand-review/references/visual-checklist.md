# Visual Checklist

## First glance
- Is the page memorable without the logo?
- Is there one dominant idea?

## Hierarchy
- What wins first, second, third?
- Does the page guide decisions?

## Noise
- Which effects add no meaning?
- Could blur/glow/shadow be reduced with no communication loss?

## Ownability
- What looks specific to this brand?
- Would the page still have character in grayscale?

## Imagery
- Does the imagery feel believable and domain-true?
- Does it support trust instead of just vibe?
