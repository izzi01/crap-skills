# Anti-Generic Visual Checklist

## First glance
- Is the page memorable without the logo?
- Is the first read clear, or just stylish?

## Hierarchy
- What wins first?
- What wins second?
- Is there one dominant hero idea?

## Noise
- Which effects are decorative only?
- Could glow, blur, or shadow be removed with no meaning loss?

## Ownability
- Which elements feel specific to this brand?
- Would the page still feel identifiable in grayscale?

## Imagery
- Does the imagery feel real, believable, and domain-true?
- Does it support trust or just vibe?

## CTA
- Is the CTA visually and structurally supported?
- Does the page guide a decision, not just showcase polish?
